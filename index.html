About

Linux User Group at UCI is for everyone interested in Linux, open-source technologies and the free software movement. We faciliate communication among fellow Linux users, and help newcomers get comfortable with Linux.

Services

Members will have access to various services provided by LUG@UCI, which will include:

  • Shell accounts
  • Web hosting
  • ... and more!
      • However, as a new organization, everything is still in flux.

FAQ

Is LUG@UCI a Recognized Campus Organization?

We are currently in the process of becoming one in a couple of weeks. Stay tuned!

How do I become a member? Is there a fee?

Glad you are interested! You can become a member by filling out our sign-up form, and there isn't a membership fee.

I'm new to Linux. Can I join?

Absolutely! We welcome people with all levels of experience. We will hold regular meetings and events to help newcomers get acquainted with Linux. Even if you don't have Linux installed, we can help!

Isn't there already a Linux User Group at UCI?

There was a Linux User Group at UCI before (around 2007), but it has been inactive ever since. We intend with revive LUG@UCI as a new organization.
